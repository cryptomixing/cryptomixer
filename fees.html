Fees

Our minimum fee is 0.5% plus 0.0005 BTC for every incoming transaction. But we advice you to set a custom fee to prevent amount-based blockchain analysis. You get a discount on service fee when you use CryptoMixer regularly. More you mix less you pay!

10 – 50 BTC 0,40% up to 0.05 BTC
50 – 100 BTC 0,35% up to 0.15 BTC
100 – 1000 BTC 0,30% up to 2.00 BTC
1000 BTC and more 0,25% 2.50 BTC / $3000+
